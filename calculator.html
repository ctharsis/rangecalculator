Assumptions:

  • At least level 140
  • All Skill Points used
  • Attack, % Attack and % Damage from passive skills included.
  • Cygnus Knights: Elemental Expert is unlocked. (Mihile included)
  • Evan: you have at least 30% MP (Dragon Fury)
  • Kaiser: you are in defensive stance (add 45 attack for offensive stance).
  • Passive stats from HYPER SKILLS (not hyper stats) are ignored.
  • This is your clean range. (no buffs)

Notes:

  • All fields must have a value, if you do not have a number, put 0
  • All Reboot Characters have a 'Hidden' 5 Magic/Attack
  • Range may be off by 1 or 2, because of Nexon Spaghetti™ code
  • The following classes have a beginner skill that grants additional attack.
    Put the additional attack under Other sources:
    Aran, Evan, Kanna
  • If Final Damage is red in the stat window, it is probably rounded some way
    or another. If you class is listed here, use the provided final damage number.
    Blaze Wizard: 87.5
